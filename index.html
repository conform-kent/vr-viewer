⚗ Lab · Experimental
Silhouette Overlay
Draws a bold outline around all model edges visible from the camera.
AO Bake Preview
Fakes screen-space ambient occlusion by darkening geometry in concave areas using a depth comparison pass.
Wall Thickness Heatmap
Casts rays inward and colours surfaces by estimated wall thickness — useful for injection-moulding review.
Draft Angle Check
Min angle 3°
Highlights faces below the minimum draft angle in red — critical for moulded plastic parts.
Mass Properties
Estimates volume, surface area, and centre of gravity from mesh geometry.
Ghost Previous Model
Overlays the previously viewed model as a transparent ghost for visual comparison.
Curvature Heatmap
Colours surfaces by mean curvature — concave in blue, convex in red, flat in white. Useful for aesthetic surface review.
Parting Line Detector
Finds and highlights the mould parting line based on the horizontal mid-plane.
